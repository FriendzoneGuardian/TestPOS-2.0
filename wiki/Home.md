## About TestPOS 2.0

TestPOS 2.0 is a modern Point of Sales web application built for farm produce retail businesses. It features:

- **Role-based access control** (Admin, Accounting, Cashier)
- **Real-time POS terminal** with product catalog, cart, and payment processing
- **Analytics & reporting** with Chart.js visualizations and CSV export
- **3 themes**: Dawn (light), Dusk (dark), AMOLED (pure black)
- **Security**: 180-second inactivity auto-logout

## Tech Stack

- Vue 3 + Vite
- Vuetify 3 (Material Design)
- Pinia state management
- Vue Router 4
- Chart.js
